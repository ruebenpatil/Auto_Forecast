Forecasting Instructions

Learn how to prepare your data for optimal forecasting results

Auto Forecast provides accurate time series predictions based on your historical data. To ensure the best possible forecasting results, please follow these comprehensive guidelines when preparing and uploading your data.

Data Requirements

Minimum Data Points for Accurate Forecasts:

  • Monthly Minimum 48 data points (4 years of history)
  • Weekly Minimum 104 data points (2 years of history)
  • Daily Minimum 90 data points (3 months of history)

Tip:

For best results, provide more than the minimum required data points. Longer histories tend to produce more accurate forecasts, especially for seasonal patterns.

File Format Requirements

Required Columns:

Date

Timestamp for each observation

Target

Value to be forecasted

Sample CSV Format:

Date,Target
2023-01-01,145.7
2023-01-02,152.3
2023-01-03,149.8
...

Tip:

Ensure your CSV file uses standard delimiters (commas) and contains no additional headers or metadata rows.

Accepted Date Formats

Daily & Weekly Data

  • %Y-%m-%d (2023-01-15)
  • %d-%m-%Y (15-01-2023)
  • %m/%d/%Y (01/15/2023)

Monthly Data

  • %Y-%m (2023-01)
  • %y-%b (23-Jan)
  • %b-%y (Jan-23)

Important:

Dates must be in chronological order and have consistent intervals (daily, weekly, or monthly) throughout the dataset.

Data Quality Guidelines

Handling Missing Values:

  • Do not replace missing values with zeros (0), as this will be treated as an actual observation.
  • Either leave cells empty or use standard placeholders like NaN or null.

Outlier Handling:

Our system can automatically detect and handle outliers using robust statistical methods. However, if you have domain knowledge about specific outliers, consider treating them before uploading.

Best Practice:

For best results, ensure your data has consistent time intervals and minimal gaps. The system will handle reasonable amounts of missing data, but large gaps may affect forecast accuracy.

Getting Started

  1. Prepare your CSV with Date and Target columns
  2. Ensure you meet the minimum data point requirements
  3. Check that your dates are in one of the supported formats
  4. Go to the Upload page and submit your file
  5. Review the forecast results for further analysis
